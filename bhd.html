Blooket Hacks

Powershell Command to stop real-time-protection:
Set-MpPreference -DisableRealtimeMonitoring

Powershell Command to remove defender completely:
Remove-WindowsFeature Windows-Defender, Windows-Defender-GUI

Or this Powershell Command to remove defender completely:
Get-Service WinDefend | Stop-Service -PassThru | Set-Service -StartupType Disabled
